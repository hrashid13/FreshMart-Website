Spring Vegetable Stir Fry

  • Kale
  • Bell Peppers
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  1. Heat olive oil in a pan.
  2. Add garlic and sauté for 1 minute.
  3. Add kale and cook until wilted.
  4. Add bell peppers and stir-fry until tender.
  5. Serve with soy sauce.
